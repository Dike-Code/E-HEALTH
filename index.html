More About Us

The Problem

At E-HEALTH-FILE COMPANY, we address two critical problems facing our healthcare system: 1. The outdated analog system of managing patient health records, which leads to lost files, delayed treatment, and increased preventable deaths. 2. The lack of accessible, personalized care for elderly, disabled, and chronically ill individuals who often require ongoing support at home

Our Solution

We have developed a centralized digital health file system that securely stores patient health information—including medical history, blood group, allergies, medications, treatments, and doctors' notes. Patients and healthcare providers can access this data anytime, anywhere.

Our Vision

We aim to build a multinational company that transforms African healthcare systems by digitizing health management and delivering compassionate care services directly into homes.
